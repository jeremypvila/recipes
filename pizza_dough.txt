Ingredients:
3 cups bread flour
2 tsp salt
2 tsp yeast
1 tsp sugar
1 1/4 cup water

Instructions:
1. Combine flour, salt, yeast, sugar, and water into a standing mixer.  Mix with dough hook until just combined.  
2. Remove from mixer and knead for 10 minutes, until firm, and dough just springs back once poked.
3. Leave in oiled boil in the fridge for 24 hours.
