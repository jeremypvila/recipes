Ingredients:
- 2 cups of flower
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp basking soda
- 1/4 tsp of salt
- 2 tbsp of sugar
- 2 cups of buttermilk
- 2 eggs
- 1 stick of butter
- 1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
1. Melt butter in a small saucepan.
2. Sift dry ingredients in a large bowl.
3. Whisk eggs and wet ingredients in a medium bowl.
4. Whisk wet ingredients into dry ingredients until just combined. Batter should be lumpy.
5. Scoop into pre-heated waffle iron.
